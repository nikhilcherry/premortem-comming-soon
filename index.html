The Executioners

Five agents. One verdict.

Each sub-agent attacks your idea from a specialized angle. There is no good cop. Only the brutal truth.

MR

The Market Realist

"Who actually paid you in the last 30 days?"

Attacks assumptions about TAM and willingness to pay. Demands cold, hard numbers.

FS

The Founder Skeptic

"Why YOU, and why now?"

Probes founder-market fit. Suspects you'll be bored in 6 months. Questions your unfair advantage.

CA

The Cost Auditor

"Show me the unit math."

Destroys ideas with bad math. Focuses on CAC, burn rate, and capital intensity.

CH

The Competitor Hunter

"What stops Notion from killing you?"

Identifies incumbents, moats, and the "good enough" status quo that wins by default.

CC

The Customer Cynic

"They're just being polite."

Attacks switching costs and integration pain. Pessimistic about user behavior change.
